One more thing

The spectrum tells you
how vivid. Not where.

Everything above - the scale from aphantasia to hyperphantasia, the test, the live results - measures a single dimension of mental imagery. But in 2024, researchers discovered there's a second, completely independent dimension that science had been overlooking entirely.

Dimension 1 - Measured above
Vividness
How bright, clear, and detailed is your mental imagery? This is what the VVIQ measures.
⬛ 🌑 🌗 🌕 ⭐

Dimension 2 - Just discovered ✦
Location
Does your imagery appear inside your mind - or overlaid on the world in front of you?
🧠 Internal External 🔭

Researchers at the University of Oxford and University of Liverpool confirmed in 2024 that some people project their mental images into their actual visual field - overlaid on the world in front of them, like augmented reality. They named this ability prophantasia. It's independent of vividness, unmeasured by any standard test, and it might explain why some people consistently score lower on the VVIQ than their imagery experience deserves.
