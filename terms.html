What this site is

The Phantasia Project is a free, independent information resource about mental imagery - specifically the spectrum from aphantasia (no voluntary mental imagery) to hyperphantasia (extremely vivid voluntary mental imagery) and prophantasia (projection of imagery into the external visual field).

The site is run by one person (JD) based in the United Kingdom. It is not a medical service, a research institution, a clinical tool, or a commercial product. Using this site means you accept these terms.

Not medical or professional advice

Nothing on this site constitutes medical, psychological, neurological, or clinical advice. The information about aphantasia, hyperphantasia, and prophantasia is provided for general educational purposes only, based on publicly available research and personal experience.

The test on this site is based on the Vividness of Visual Imagery Questionnaire (VVIQ), a widely used psychometric tool, but it is not a clinical assessment and your result is not a diagnosis of any condition. If you have concerns about your cognitive health or mental imagery, please speak to a qualified healthcare professional.

The research information on this site is summarised for a general audience. It is not a substitute for reading the primary sources, which are linked throughout. Research in this area is evolving rapidly and some information may become outdated.
